About Matt Mueller

Born and raised in southern Minnesota, Matt Mueller grew up surrounded by music, family, and life on a small hobby farm. From an early age, he participated in band, choir, and musicals, discovering both a love for performing and the joy music brings to others.

At 14, Matt received his first guitar, teaching himself to play and experimenting with songwriting. After high school in 2020, he joined the Minnesota Army National Guard while continuing to pursue music.

In 2022, Matt began performing solo shows, which eventually led to forming a band. In 2023, he co-founded Whiskey Peddler, delivering energetic, professional-quality performances on the local circuit, and also graduated from college.

In 2025, Matt released his debut single and continues to perform both with Whiskey Peddler and as a solo artist. Drawing inspiration from diverse genres, he writes heartfelt songs that blend personal storytelling with a sound uniquely his own.
